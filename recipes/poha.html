Poha

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice (poha): 2 cups
  • Onion: 1 (chopped)
  • Green chilies: 2 (chopped)
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Peanuts: 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Rinse poha and drain water.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and peanuts.
  3. Saut√© onions and green chilies. Add poha and salt; mix well.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with coriander and lemon.
