SARVA PINDI

Ingredients

  • Rice flour: 2 cups
  • Chana dal: 2 tbsp (soaked)
  • Green chilies: 2-3 (chopped)
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Sesame seeds: 1 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil: for frying

Steps

  1. 1. Mix rice flour, soaked chana dal, green chilies, curry leaves, sesame seeds, and salt into a thick dough.
  2. 2. Take a portion, flatten it on a greased pan, and make holes in the dough.
  3. 3. Drizzle oil and cook on medium flame until crisp on both sides.
  4. 4. Serve with pickle or chutney.
