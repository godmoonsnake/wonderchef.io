Chettinad Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g
  • Onion: 2 (chopped)
  • Tomato: 2 (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tbsp
  • Chettinad masala: 3 tbsp
  • Coconut paste: 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Oil: 3 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and curry leaves.
  2. Add chicken, turmeric, and salt; cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and Chettinad masala; cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in coconut paste and simmer until chicken is tender.
  5. Serve hot with rice or parotta.
