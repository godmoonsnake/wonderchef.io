Thepla

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour: 2 cups
  • Fenugreek leaves (methi): 1 cup (chopped)
  • Turmeric: Â½ tsp
  • Red chili powder: 1 tsp
  • Yogurt: 2 tbsp
  • Oil: for cooking

Steps

  1. Knead wheat flour, fenugreek, spices, and yogurt into a dough.
  2. Roll into thin flatbreads.
  3. Cook on a hot tawa with a drizzle of oil until golden. Serve with pickle or yogurt.
