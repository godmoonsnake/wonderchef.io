Goan Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • Fish (pomfret or mackerel): 500g
  • Coconut milk: 2 cups
  • Tamarind pulp: 2 tbsp
  • Red chili powder: 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder: ½ tsp
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Oil: 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  2. Add red chili powder, turmeric, and tamarind pulp; stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add fish and cook for 10 minutes. Serve hot with rice.
