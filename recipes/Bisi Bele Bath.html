bisi bele bath

Ingredients

  • • Rice: 1 cup
    • • Toor dal: ½ cup • Tamarind pulp: 2 tbsp • Vegetables (carrot, beans, peas): 1 cup (chopped) • Bisi Bele Bath powder: 2 tbsp • Ghee: 2 tbsp • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp • Curry leaves: 1 sprig • Cashews: 10 • Salt: to taste 3

Steps

  1. 1. Cook rice and toor dal separately.
  2. 2. Boil vegetables with a pinch of salt until tender.
  3. 3. In a large pot, combine cooked rice, dal, vegetables, and tamarind pulp.
  4. 4. Add Bisi Bele Bath powder and simmer for 10 minutes, adding water to adjust consistency.
  5. 5. Temper with ghee, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and cashews. Pour over the dish.
  6. 6. Serve hot with papad or raita.
