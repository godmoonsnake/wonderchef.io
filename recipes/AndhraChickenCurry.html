Andhra Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g
  • Onion: 2 (finely chopped)
  • Green chilies: 3-4
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Red chili powder: 1 tbsp
  • Coriander powder: 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder: ½ tsp
  • Garam masala: 1 tsp
  • Tamarind pulp: 2 tbsp
  • Oil: 3 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • Coriander leaves: for garnish

Steps

  1. 1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. 2. Add green chilies, curry leaves, and ginger-garlic paste; cook until the raw smell disappears.
  3. 3. Add chicken, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Sauté for 5-7 minutes.
  4. 4. Add water to cover the chicken and cook for 15 minutes.
  5. 5. Stir in tamarind pulp and garam masala. Simmer for 10 more minutes.
  6. 6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
