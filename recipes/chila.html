Chila

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 cup (soaked and ground to batter)
  • Green chilies: 2 (chopped)
  • Onion: 1 (chopped)
  • Salt: to taste

Steps

  1. Mix rice batter, chilies, onion, and salt.
  2. Pour a ladle of batter onto a hot pan and cook both sides until crisp.
  3. Serve with chutney or pickle.
