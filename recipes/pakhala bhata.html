pakhala bhata

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice: 2 cups
  • Water: 3 cups
  • Curd: Â½ cup
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix rice, water, and curd in a bowl. Let it ferment overnight.
  2. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves. Serve with fried vegetables or fish.
