Bhutte Ka Kees

Ingredients

  • Fresh corn: 2 cups (grated)
  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Green chilies: 2
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Ghee: 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Heat ghee and saut√© mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies.
  2. Add grated corn, cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour milk, stir, and simmer until thick. Serve hot.
