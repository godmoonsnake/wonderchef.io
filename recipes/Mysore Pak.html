mysore pak

Ingredients

  • Ghee: 2 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • • Gram flour (besan): 1 cup
  • • Sugar: 2 cups
  • • Ghee: 1 cup
  • • Water: ½ cup

Steps

  1. 1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, mint, coriander, and biryani masala for 2 hours
  2. 2. Parboil rice with whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf,etc)
  3. 3. Layer marinated chicken and rice in a pot, adding fried onions, saffron milk, and ghee.
  4. 4. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 30-40 minutes.
  5. 5. Serve with raita or mirchi ka salan.
