idli with chutney

Ingredients

  • Idli batter: 2 cups
  • Fresh coconut: 1 cup (grated)
  • Green chilies: 2
  • Ginger: Â½ inch
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Oil: 1 tsp
  • Salt: to taste

Steps

  1. Steam idlis using an idli steamer for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Grind coconut, green chilies, ginger, and salt into a smooth chutney.
  3. Temper chutney with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  4. Serve idlis with chutney.
