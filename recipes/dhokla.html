Dhokla

Ingredients

  • Gram flour: 2 cups
  • Yogurt: 1 cup
  • Turmeric: Â¼ tsp
  • Eno fruit salt: 1 tsp
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Green chilies: 2 (slit)
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix gram flour, yogurt, turmeric, and water into a smooth batter. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Add Eno and steam the batter in a greased plate for 20 minutes.
  3. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies; pour over the dhokla. Serve with chutney.
