Kerala Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 1.Fish: 500g
  • Tamarind pulp: 2 tbsp
  • Coconut milk: 1 cup
  • Red chili powder: 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder: ½ tsp
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Oil: 2 tbsp

Steps to Prepare

  1. 1.Marinate fish with turmeric and salt.
  2. 2.Heat oil and sauté curry leaves and chili powder.
  3. 3.Add tamarind pulp and water; bring to a boil.
  4. 4.Add fish and simmer until cooked. Stir in coconut milk and serve with rice.
