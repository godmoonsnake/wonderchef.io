Appam with Stew

Ingredients

For Appam:

  • Rice flour: 2 cups
  • Coconut milk: 1 cup
  • Yeast: ½ tsp
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste

For Stew:

  • Vegetables or chicken: 2 cups
  • Coconut milk: 2 cups
  • Onion: 1 (sliced)
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece (sliced)
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Oil: 2 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste

Steps

For Appam:

  1. Activate yeast in warm water with sugar.
  2. Mix rice flour, yeast mixture, and coconut milk to form a batter. Let it ferment for 6-8 hours.
  3. Pour batter into a hot appam pan, swirl, and cook with the lid on.

For Stew:

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions, ginger, and curry leaves.
  2. Add vegetables or chicken with salt and water; cook until tender.
  3. Stir in coconut milk and simmer. Serve with appam.
