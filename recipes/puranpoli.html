Puran Poli

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour: 2 cups
  • Jaggery: 1 cup
  • Chana dal: 1 cup
  • Cardamom powder: Â½ tsp
  • Ghee: for cooking

Steps

  1. Cook chana dal until soft and mash it. Mix with jaggery and cardamom powder.
  2. Knead wheat flour with water into a soft dough.
  3. Roll out dough, stuff with dal mixture, and roll again into a flatbread.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle with ghee. Serve warm.
