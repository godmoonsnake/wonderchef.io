pesarattu

Ingredients

  • Green gram (moong dal): 1 cup (soaked overnight)
  • • Rice: 2 tbsp (soaked overnight)
  • • Ginger: 1-inch piece
  • • Green chilies: 2-3
  • • Cumin seeds: 1 tsp
  • • Salt: to taste
  • • Oil: for cooking

Steps

  1. 1. Blend soaked green gram, rice, ginger, green chilies, cumin, and salt into a smooth batter.
  2. 2. Heat a tawa and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it thin like a dosa.
  3. 3. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until crisp.
  4. 4. Serve hot with coconut or ginger chutney.
