Ingredients

  • 1 cup sattu (roasted gram flour)
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1 tsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 brinjal (eggplant)
  • 1 potato (boiled)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Mix sattu, ajwain, salt, and mustard oil. Add water to form a dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls and cook them in a tandoor or bake them until golden and crispy.
  3. For chokha, roast the brinjal and potato, peel off the skin, and mash them together with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and salt.
  4. Serve the litti with chokha and a drizzle of mustard oil.
