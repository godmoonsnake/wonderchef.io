kadhipakoda

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare the pakoras by mixing gram flour, water, and salt. Form small fritters and deep fry until golden.
  2. For the kadhi, heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add yogurt, water, turmeric, and red chili powder to the pan, and bring it to a boil.
  4. Add the fried pakoras and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice.
