chole bhature

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for bhature)
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight, cook in a pressure cooker until soft.
  2. For chole, heat oil, add cumin seeds, and saut√© onions. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  3. Add tomatoes, spices, and cooked chickpeas. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. For bhature, prepare dough with flour, yeast, sugar, and water. Let it rise.
  5. Roll out bhature and deep fry until puffed.
  6. Serve hot with chole and pickle.
