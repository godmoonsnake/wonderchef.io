momo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • Filling ingredients (choose one or a combination):
    • Ground beef or pork
    • Chopped vegetables (onion, cabbage, carrot)
    • Paneer or tofu

      • Spices for filling:

    • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
    • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
    • Salt, to taste
  • 1 cup roasted red chilies
  • 1/2 cup roasted garlic
  • 1/2 cup roasted ginger
  • 1/4 cup mustard oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Make the dough: Combine flour, salt, and baking powder in a bowl. Add ghee or oil and mix until the dough comes together. Gradually add lukewarm water and knead for 5-7 minutes. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling: Mix chosen filling ingredients with ginger paste, garlic paste, soy sauce, chili flakes, and salt.
  3. Assemble the momos: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a thin circle. Place a tablespoon or two of the filling in the center. Fold the dough over the filling and press the edges together to seal.
  4. Steam the momos: Place the momos in a steamer basket, leaving some space between each. Steam for 10-15 minutes or until the dough is cooked through.
  5. Make the chutney: Blend roasted red chilies, garlic, ginger, mustard oil, lemon juice, and salt into a smooth paste. Add minced garlic, if using.
  6. Serve: Serve the steamed momos with the spicy chutney.
