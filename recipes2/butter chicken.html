butter chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 100ml cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, and lemon juice for 1-2 hours.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, saut√© chopped onions till golden.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  4. Add marinated chicken, cook until browned and tender.
  5. Add cream, salt, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with naan or rice.
