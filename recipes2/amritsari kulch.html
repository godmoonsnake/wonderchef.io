amritsari kulcha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated paneer
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves
  • Butter for brushing

Steps

  1. Activate yeast in warm water with sugar and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Mix flour, salt, and yeast mixture to form a soft dough. Let it rest for an hour.
  3. Divide dough into balls and stuff with paneer, onions, and coriander leaves.
  4. Roll into flat discs, then cook on a hot griddle or tandoor.
  5. Brush with butter and serve with pickle and yogurt.
