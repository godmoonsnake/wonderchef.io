Atte Ka Malpua Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 2-3 tablespoons semolina
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • 1/2 cup milk (or as needed)
  • 1/4 cup mawa (optional, for richness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel powder
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cream
  • Ghee (for frying)
  • Pistachio slivers and dried rose petals (for garnish)

    • >For Sugar Syrup:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands

Steps

    Steps for Sugar Syrup:

  1. Combine the sugar, water, cardamom powder, and saffron strands in a deep non-stick pan.
  2. Cook on a medium flame for 5 minutes, stirring continuously.
  3. Keep aside to cool slightly.

    4. Steps for Making Malpua:

  4. In a deep bowl, add whole wheat flour, semolina, mawa, cardamom powder, fennel powder, fresh cream, and milk.
  5. Mix well to form a smooth, lump-free batter.
  6. Heat ghee in a broad non-stick pan.
  7. Pour a ladleful of the mixture into the hot ghee, one at a time.
  8. Deep-fry on a medium flame until golden brown on both sides.
  9. Drain well and dip each malpua in the warm sugar syrup for 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. Repeat to make 12 more malpuas.
  11. Garnish with pistachio slivers and dried rose petals.
  12. Serve the malpua immediately.
