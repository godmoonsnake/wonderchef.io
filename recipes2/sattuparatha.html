sattu paratha

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sattu
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (chopped)
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Ghee for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix wheat flour, sattu, chopped onions, chilies, coriander, and salt.
  2. Knead into a dough using water as needed.
  3. Roll the dough into flat parathas and cook on a tawa with ghee until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve with pickle or yogurt.
