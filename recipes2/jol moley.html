Jol Moley

Ingredients

  • 500 gm fish pieces (preferably Rohu or Catla)
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 medium-sized onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 1 teaspoon panch phoron (Assamese five-spice mix)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the panch phoron: Dry roast the fennel seeds, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, cumin seeds, and coriander seeds in a pan until fragrant. Let cool, then grind into a fine powder.
  2. Marinate the fish: Mix the fish pieces with salt, lemon juice, and chopped green chilies. Let marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Make the curry: Heat mustard oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add spices and fish: Add the panch phoron powder and marinated fish pieces to the pan. Mix well.
  5. Add water and simmer: Add 2 cups of water to the pan and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the fish is cooked through.
  6. Season and garnish: Season the curry with salt to taste. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with steamed rice.
