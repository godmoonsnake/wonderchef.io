Jadoh

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 300g pork (cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© the pork pieces until browned.
  3. Add onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and cook until soft.
  4. Mix the cooked rice with the pork mixture and cook together for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
