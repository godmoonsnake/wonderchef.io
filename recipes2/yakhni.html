yakhni

Ingredients

  • Ingredients:
  • 500g mutton
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat ghee and add whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaf).
  2. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add mutton, brown it lightly, then add water.
  5. Cook on medium heat for about an hour, until the meat is tender.
  6. Add yogurt and cook for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with rice.
